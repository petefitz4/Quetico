Trip Details

The trip will be scheduled over a 6 day period in summer 2018. There is an 9 person limit per group to enter Quetico National Park

The car trip is 9 hours. We propose leaving at noon on a Wednesday with a late night arrival in Ely, MN. Launch in the waters on Thursday and exit Monday. Start a road trip back on Monday and arrive in E-town late night Monday or Tuesday.

Proposed dates

  • June 27 - July 2
  • July 5 - July 11
  • July 11 - July 17

Proposed Costs per person

  • Roadtrip: $25
  • Outfitter daily rental: $100 (includes canoe, camping gear, food, fees, transportation)
